Features and Benefits

INSTITUTIONAL ACCESS

We source large volumes of institutional grade real estate that offers attractive risk-adjusted yields. Sponsor focused lending philosophy. Emphasis on liquidity, experience, creditworthiness, and net worth.

FIXED INCOME

We invest in diversified, liquid assets in strong tier-one metros. Short-term loans to reduce market and duration risk. Secured first trust deeds. Quarterly interest distributions to investors.

DEFENSIVE ASSET CLASS

We focus on hard real estate assets rooted by strong credit and underwriting principles Responsible Loan to Value (LTV) Rigorous collateral monitoring and control of loan disbursements. Prudent use of leverage, used to minimize “cash drag” and maximize investor returns. Focus on experienced sponsors and operators with proven track records and financial strength

DIVERSIFICATION

Real estate debt returns are largely uncorrelated to mainstream asset classes allowing diversification in a multi-asset portfolio.
