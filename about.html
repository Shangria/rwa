ABOUT RWA GROUP

Realworld Asset Group is a real estate fund led by a seasoned management team with a proven track record of providing creative funding solutions to professional real estate project sponsors/operators and delivering superior risk-adjusted returns to investors. With over 30+ years of combined experience managing, operating, and generating loan volume for high-performing private real estate debt funds.

CHECK OUT THE TEAM

We are investors, builders & innovators across the real estate and technology spectrum.

JUSTIN MITCHELL

Justin’s investment approach prioritizes liquidity, high-quality operators, and capital preservation, establishing him as a world-class real estate lender and operator. Before founding RWA Group, Justin was a co-founder at Quanta Finance, a top 5 national private credit mortgage lender in the US and UK with over 3,500 loans originated for a total loan volume of $2.6B+, and zero loss of principal capital.

Previously, Justin was VP of Analytics (Employee #5) at Genesis Capital, a high-growth specialty finance company that made bridge loans to residential real estate developers. Genesis Capital was backed by Oaktree Capital Management, one of the world's premier money managers focused on lending and real estate markets, and was acquired by Goldman Sachs.

Justin started his Real Estate career in 2007 at Countrywide as an analyst on the Secondary Markets team. After Bank of America acquired Countrywide, Justin was a senior analyst on BofA’s Credit Risk Management team, which he helped grow from a small team of 25 to over 3,000 headcount post-GFC.

KURT DARADICS

Prior to Co-Founder & COO, Kurt has honed his craft of go-to-market, business development, and team building with high-level executive and leadership experience at tech companies including Esri, Lime, Hunt Club, as well as being a founder (CitySourced, acquired in a Vista backed PE roll up).

Kurt has experienced the continuum of problem-solution fit, product-market fit, growth, and scale. Kurt has a reputation for helping organizations make positive commercial and social impacts in the world.

Prior to a 15 year career in tech, Kurt started his career with five years in the real estate sector as a mortgage broker, until he transitioned to tech in 2008.

Kurt studied Mass Communication at UCSB and has a CA Real Estate License

ELIAS BADIN

Prior to joining RWA Group, Elias Badin was the Vice President, Finance at Transom Capital, a mid market Private Equity Firm, where Elias partnered with the executive leadership of Transom’s portfolio companies on finance-related activities, including financial reporting and compliance, cash flow management, and the successful execution of long-term strategic initiatives designed to generate enterprise value by delivering cross-functional operating efficiencies.

Previously, Elias was a consultant at NOW CFO, where he advised and supported companies with limited accounting and finance functions by providing strategic leadership, subject matter expertise and transactional support.

Elias also served as Director of Accounting for FTD Companies’ ProFlowers division. Elias started his career in accounting at PwC where he spent 5 years in the alternative investment practice consulting hedge funds and private equity clients in the assurance practice.

Elias holds a Bachelor of Science in Accounting and Business Administration from the University of San Diego and is a former CPA.
